Policies & Guidelines

Please familiarize yourself with our policies to ensure a pleasant stay for all guests:

  • Non-Smoking Policy: Our property is 100% smoke-free. A cleaning fee of $250 will be charged for smoking in non-designated areas.
  • Pet Policy: We welcome well-behaved pets under 30 pounds with a one-time fee of $75 per stay.
  • Cancellation Policy: Cancellations must be made 48 hours prior to arrival to avoid a one-night charge.
  • Quiet Hours: 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM to ensure all guests can enjoy a peaceful rest.
  • Extra Person Fee: $25 per person, per night for guests beyond double occupancy.
  • Parking: Valet parking is available for $30 per night with in-and-out privileges.
  • Lost Key Cards: A $20 fee will be charged for lost key cards.
